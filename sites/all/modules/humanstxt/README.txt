Helper module to place and manage a humans.txt file in your site.
Just download the module, place in modules folder and enable it.
-------------------
What is humans.txt?
-------------------
It's an initiative for knowing the people behind a website.
It's a TXT file that contains information about the different people who have 
contributed to building the website.
-------------
Why should I?
-------------
You don't have to if you don't want. The only aim of this initiative is to know
who the authors of the sites we visit are.

More information on http://http://humanstxt.org/
